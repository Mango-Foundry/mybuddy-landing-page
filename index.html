Privacy Policy

Last update: 22 November 2025

Thank you for using myBuddy (the "App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to improve our product for our users. This information includes usage patterns, device model, operating system version, app version and crash reports. This information is collected anonymously. We do not collect information that can be used to identify you.

2. How We Use The Information

We use Google's Firebase Analytics to help us understand how the App is used. This may include the screens or pages you view, and the currencies you select. The information we collect is used to improve the App’s performance and reliability, understand general usage patterns, and address technical issues.

3. Crash Reports

We use Google Firebase Crashlytics to help detect and fix technical issues. When a crash or error occurs, Firebase Crashlytics automatically collects diagnostic information such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Technical details about the error

This helps us improve the App's performance and stability.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, Google Firebase Analytics, Google Firebase Crashlytics, Google Cloud API.  These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Firebase Privacy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Cloud Privacy: https://cloud.google.com/terms/cloud-privacy-notice

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
