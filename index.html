The Story of myBuddy

I first arrived in Cambodia in 2022. I was looking for a quiet place to learn to code, and maybe build my first app. What I didn’t expect was how much this place, especially the animals, would shape that journey.

Like many first-time visitors, I found the dual-currency system confusing. US dollars and Cambodian riel (KHR) are used side by side, and figuring out change in two currencies quickly became a daily puzzle. I found myself fumbling through conversions in cafes, bars, and markets... and I noticed I wasn’t the only one.

That’s why I created myBuddy... The Travel App for Cambodia. It started as a simple tool to help visitors understand and convert between USD, KHR, and a third currency of their choice. But this is just the beginning. There’s so much to discover in this wonderful country, and the goal is to keep adding features to myBuddy that make travel in Cambodia smoother, easier, and more enjoyable.

The app is named after Buddy, the first of several stray cats who found their way into my life here. Since then, I’ve become more aware of the tough lives many street dogs and cats face across the country. If myBuddy finds success, I hope to give back by supporting local animal welfare initiatives.

This is my first published app. I made it for first-time visitors to Cambodia and travelers like me... curious about the country, slightly lost at first, but slowly exploring its wonders.
