myBuddy Cambodia

The Travel App for Cambodia

A simple travel tool to help you navigate the dual-currency system in Cambodia, giving you peace of mind.

Money works a little differently in Cambodia...

  • Cambodia uses a dual-currency system: US dollars (USD) and Cambodian riel (KHR).
  • Prices may be shown in either currency or both.
  • Payments can be made with dollars or riel, or a mix of both.
  • Change is often given in both currencies.

For visitors, this often means converting between dollars, riel, and your own currency... sometimes all in the same transaction.

How this app helps...

  • Instantly convert between US dollar, Cambodian riel and your selected currency.
  • Includes common rate option used by many local shops and street vendors.
  • Works offline, wherever you are paying.
  • Learn the value of Cambodian notes in your own currency.

myBuddy is designed for real-world cash use in Cambodia...
easy to use, giving you confidence and peace of mind.
